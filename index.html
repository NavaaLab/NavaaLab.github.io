Welcome to Navaa Lab

This is the homepage of Navaa Lab – iraNiAn music retrieVal & Analysis

Navaa Lab is dedicated to the exploration, analysis, and computational modeling of Iranian music. We specialize in Music Information Retrieval (MIR) techniques tailored to the unique characteristics of Iran’s rich musical heritage.

Our mission is to:

  • Develop and publish open datasets on Iranian music
  • Propose models and tools that respect and understand the nuances of Iranian music theory and performance
  • Foster reproducible research in underrepresented musical traditions

What We Do:

  • Music analysis using MIR techniques
  • Dataset curation and annotation
  • Cross-cultural computational musicology
  • Research publications and software tools
  • Promotion of open, inclusive research in regional music traditions
